Our Partner NGOs

RuralCare Foundation

RuralCare Foundation focuses on improving rural living standards by offering healthcare support, sanitation programs, and educational assistance. They conduct regular health camps, distribute hygiene kits, and promote awareness on nutrition and preventive healthcare.

In education, they work with local schools to provide books, uniforms, digital learning tools, and after-school tutoring. They aim to empower rural families through sustainable community development.

EduBridge Trust

EduBridge Trust works to ensure equal access to education for children in underprivileged communities. Their programs include scholarships, free tuition classes, study material distribution, and mentoring sessions for students.

The trust also collaborates with schools to introduce digital learning, teacher training workshops, and career guidance for young learners.

Women Rise Initiative

Women Rise Initiative empowers women through skill development, entrepreneurship training, and awareness programs. They conduct courses in tailoring, crafts, computer basics, and beauty services to support women becoming financially independent.

They also hold sessions on women's rights, health, safety, and financial literacy, helping women gain confidence and leadership qualities within their communities.
